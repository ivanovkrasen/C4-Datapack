Each mark requires its own trial.
The Mark of the Scholar is reserved for those who pass the Trial of the Scholar. You must be above level 35 to qualify. To take this test visit Magister Mirien in the temple of Dion Castle Town.
The Mark of Life is given to those who pass the Testimony of Life. You must be above level 37 to take the test. To take this test visit Master Cardien of Dion Castle Town.
And last, the Mark of the Summoner is reserved for those who pass the Test of the Summoner. You must be above level 39 to qualify. To take this test visit High Summoner Galatea of Gludin.

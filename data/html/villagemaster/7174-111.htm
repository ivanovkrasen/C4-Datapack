Spell Singers are somewhat similar to Human Sorcerers, but with Elven sensibilities. They practice a potent form of elemental magic. Very fast during combat and more efficient and safe than other Mages. Most dreaded opponent during a siege.
To become a Spell Singer, your level must be over 40 and you must collect the Mark of the Scholar, the Mark of Life and the Mark of the Magus.
